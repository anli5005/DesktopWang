Desktop Wang

The Man, The Myth, The Legend

Project Info:

Hello there! We are a team of three from Bergen County Academies, and this is our final project, Desktop Wang. We were inspired by the fear of our beloved computer science teacher (Mr.Wang) leaving our school, as well as the beauty of the app Desktop Goose. We built it to test our knowledge of JavaFX, and now the final product is avaliable to you for free! Just press the download button above to download!

Project Description:
